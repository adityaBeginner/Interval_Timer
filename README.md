# Interval Timer Pro

Interval Timer Pro is a powerful training and event management application designed for athletes, coaches, and fitness enthusiasts. The app helps users create structured interval workouts, reaction drills, and timed events with customizable work periods, rest intervals, audio cues, and motivational messages.

## Features

* Create and manage custom interval training sessions.
* Configure work durations, rest periods, rounds, and total workout time.
* Schedule athlete-specific events and challenges with defined completion timeframes.
* Play custom sounds, songs, and voice prompts during workouts.
* Create reaction timers for speed, agility, and reflex training.
* Organize workouts using a folder-based management system.
* Import and export timer configurations using CSV files.
* Generate interval timers from CSV templates for quick setup.
* Share workout plans and timer configurations with other users.
* Customizable themes and color settings for a personalized experience.
* Support for multiple training formats including HIIT, Tabata, Circuit Training, Sports Conditioning, and Recovery Sessions.
* Progress tracking and event completion monitoring.

## Use Cases

* Athletic training programs
* Sports team conditioning
* Personal fitness routines
* CrossFit and HIIT workouts
* Reaction and reflex training
* Coaching and group training sessions
* Event-based fitness challenges

Built with flexibility and performance in mind, Interval Timer Pro enables athletes and trainers to create, manage, and share professional training sessions with ease.
